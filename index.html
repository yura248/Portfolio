Epilogue

When I first started out, I was a beginner who didn't even know how to use basic tools like Photoshop or Illustrator. The idea of having to learn ten different things from scratch was daunting, and I had many concerns. However, as I gradually learned and completed my own projects one by one, I began to find design more and more enjoyable.

Among these projects, creating my portfolio was a task that required a lot of effort, as I wanted to showcase everything I had learned and created up to that point. Though the process of constantly adding and refining my work was not easy, I felt a sense of amazement and fulfillment when I saw the end result and remembered how I was when I first started.

While I am still a novice designer with much more to learn than what I have already learned, I aspire to become a designer who always strives to do their best and produce work that they are not ashamed of. Thank you.
